Current Projects

While studying at Allegheny College all seniors are required to work on a senior project. For my senior comprehensive project I built a website that allows people to add greater depth and layers to flashcard learning.

Multilayered Flashcards, as the name suggests is a flashcards learning application. Multilayered Flashcards is set apart because rather than having just one item on the front and one item on the back, Multilayered Flashcards allows the user to have more than two items of study and allows the user to choose which pieces of information they would like of the front and back of their flashcard. At the beginning of each study session they can select the options that they want on the front and on the back by using the Options Menu. There are multiple different data sets available for use. These study sets include Mandarin, Greek Alphabet, Hebrew Alphabet, Arabic Alphabet, Periodic Table, Animal Information, American Presidents, and Roman Emperor study sets. And there are more study sets to come.
